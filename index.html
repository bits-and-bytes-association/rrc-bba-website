Bits and Bytes Association

Winter 2018

The Bits & Bytes Association (BBA) was created to improve communication between students and staff to enhance the quality of education for Introduction to Business Information Technology (IBIT), Business Information Technology (BIT), Business Technology Management (BTM) and Information Security (IS) programs offered at Red River College through the Applied Computer Education (ACE) department. Since its inception in 2015, BBA has grown to over 100 members today and continutes to climb steadily. If you would like to join the Bits and Bytes, You can join our Discord!

This semster the BBA has plans to host a social after final exams are over. This is a big event we are planning and any help would be greatly appreciated.

If you are intresting in getting involved with the Bits and Bytes, we meet every Monday at 2:00pm - 3:00pm in room W-315.

These meetings are open to all and anyone can attend.
