Online Course Testing

Red River College is currently developing online versions of the Programming 1 (for BIT and BTM) and Introduction to Programming Logic (IBIT and BTM). Katherine Coley is looking for student volunteers to help test and evaluate these courses. Some of the aspects being tested for include:

  • Is there any missing/unecessary content?
  • Are there any errors in the content?
  • Are there any confusing gaps?
  • Does the content teach enough to do the assignments?
  • Does the content flow in logical order?
  • Any other innovative ideas or suggestions?

Content is broken down into several small modules, so you get to control your level of commitment. Picking up just one or two modules can go a long way!

People interested in volunteering can contact Katherine at kcoley@rrc.ca
